About Me

I'm a creative at heart who loves to bring visions to life, whether it's in the digital sphere through code or physical mediums in my artwork. I also occasionally explore design work. When I'm not debugging a project or sketching out a new idea, I enjoy reading, travelling and podcasting!

I am currently an undergraduate at Clemson University majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Digital Production Arts. My current interests are front-end development, data visualization, machine learning, and data science.While at Clemson, I have done research in virtual reality, worked as a mentor to women in STEM with PEER/WISE, and help annually as an organizer for Cuhackit, Clemson's premiere hackathon.

I strive to engage in meaningful and fulfilling work with an emphasis on impact, whether it's at the local, national, or global scale. My current interests lie in front-end development, machine learning, data visualization and data science.
